Switzerland


The snow-covered Swiss Alps and the scenic lakes amidst the mountainous backdrops are something straight out of an artist’s imagination. The beauty is beyond words. Mark Twain, the famous author, writing about his Switzerland travel experience in A Tramp Abroad, said that “…The scenery is beyond comparison beautiful. Sunday in heaven is noisy compared to this quietness.”
